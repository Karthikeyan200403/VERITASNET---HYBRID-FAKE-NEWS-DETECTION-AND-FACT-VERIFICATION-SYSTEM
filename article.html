Climate Scientists Confirm: No Evidence of a New Ice Age

Source: Global News Network • Published Oct 28, 2025

Recent viral posts claim that Earth is entering a new ice age. However, leading climate organizations confirm that current data does not support this claim.

The IPCC reports continued warming trends, and no scientific data supports the ice age claim.
